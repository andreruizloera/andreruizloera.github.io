About this site

A record and a workshop, not a brochure.

This is the canonical home page for Andre Ruiz Loera. It exists to represent the work, the reading, and the thinking in one calm, ad-free space that outlasts any platform feed. Rather than asserting depth, it shows it — through structured reading notes, curated lists, and writing that earns its place. Below is what you will find here.

What you'll find here

Five surfaces, one consistent voice.

Each links into the existing site. Some are richly populated; others are growing as real items land.

Blog

Fewer, better essays on mathematics, markets, and craft. Each post earns its place; quality over cadence.

Annotations

The intellectual core: structured reading notes across books, math, finance, and philosophy, with real LaTeX rendered properly.
